Seasonal Crops Guide

Wheat

  • Sowing: October-November
  • Harvesting: March-April
  • Ideal Temperature: 20-25°C
  • Water Requirement: Moderate

Wheat requires well-drained loamy soil with pH between 5.5-7.5. It's a rabi crop that thrives in cool weather.

Rice

  • Sowing: June-July
  • Harvesting: October-November
  • Ideal Temperature: 20-35°C
  • Water Requirement: High

Rice requires clayey or loamy soil that can retain water. It's a kharif crop that needs high temperature and humidity.

Corn

  • Sowing: June-July
  • Harvesting: September-October
  • Ideal Temperature: 18-27°C
  • Water Requirement: Moderate

Corn grows best in well-drained soils with pH between 5.5-7.0. It requires ample sunlight and warm weather.
